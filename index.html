About BHR Custom Homes

Craftsmanship
you can see.
Quality you'll live in.

BHR Custom Homes started as Bright Home Repair and grew into something bigger — because our clients kept coming back with bigger visions. Today we are a licensed general contractor focused on custom builds, full-scale luxury remodels, and additions that change how you experience your home.

Owner Jeff Wyner brings over 20 years of hands-on experience to every project. That means no subcontractors cutting corners, no surprises at the finish line, and a final product you're proud to live in.

Jeff Wyner
Owner & General Contractor
