What We Build

We build the home you've always dreamed of coming home to.

Custom Home Builds

After an initial consultation, we walk your building site and work through every detail — from structural materials to finish selections — before a single tool touches the ground. We are committed to clear, consistent communication that keeps you fully informed at every phase, whether you're local or building from out of state.

As a fixed-bid contractor, BHR Custom Homes provides a fair price and a firm timeline before breaking ground. You know exactly what you're getting before we start, and we deliver exactly what we promised when we finish.

Luxury Remodels

Kitchen, bathroom, basement, and whole-home remodels built to transform how you live every day. We approach every remodel the same way we approach a new build — thorough planning upfront, skilled execution on-site, and a finished product that exceeds what you imagined when you first called us.

Home Additions

Expanding your home's footprint is one of the best investments you can make — when it's done right. BHR designs and builds additions that feel like they were always part of the original plan. We match existing materials, maintain structural integrity, and deliver an addition that adds real value to your home and your life.

Commercial Renovations

Tenant improvements and commercial builds executed on schedule and on budget. We understand that time is money in a commercial space — we keep your business moving and your project on track, with the same attention to detail we bring to every residential project.

About

Our Team

Jeff Wyner
Owner & General Contractor

Jeff Wyner started building homes over 20 years ago and has spent every year since refining the craft. What began as Bright Home Repair — a handyman service built on showing up, doing the work right, and never making excuses — grew naturally into BHR Custom Homes as clients kept coming back with bigger projects and bigger visions.

Jeff oversees every project from conception to completion. He is on-site, day-to-day, solving problems before they become delays and making sure the work reflects the standard he has spent two decades building his reputation on. When you choose BHR, you have a builder who is your advocate from the first handshake — not a project manager you've never met.

Licensed and insured. Straight-talking. Driven by results.
